Prof. K. Ratnabali is a distinguished legal scholar and policy expert specializing in constitutional law, tribal rights, international humanitarian law, and legal education reforms. She serves as a Professor at Law Centre-I, Faculty of Law, University of Delhi, and as the Dean of Academic Affairs, where she has been shaping academic policies for over two decades.

Her Ph.D. research on “Land as a Cultural and Religious Space for Tribals” has significantly contributed to understanding tribal rights and land laws. She has authored multiple books and research articles, and her expertise is recognized through invitations as a keynote speaker at national and international conferences.

As a leader in implementing the National Education Policy (NEP) 2020, she chairs the NEP Cell and has served on UGC committees for multiple entry/exit systems and national credit frameworks. Her dedication to social justice and academic excellence continues to inspire students and policymakers.
